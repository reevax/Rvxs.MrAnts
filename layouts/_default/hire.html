Private Function Hire

Got something to celebrate?

Mr Ant’s is available for your functions free of charge with a full bar service for groups of over 30 on Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday after 3pm and Sunday day and evening.

Smaller groups may have free hire of their own roped off VIP area for their exclusive use with table service.

Sole hire of Mr Ant’s is available for smaller groups but a hire charge will be applicable dependent on numbers.

Also available:

  • Select your own cocktail menu
  • Playlist of your choice
  • Snacks and buffet menu available as mobile bar service

Private Hire

  • Confirmation and Deposit

    A written confirmation and refundable deposit of £100 is required for sole hire. Final confirmation is required one week in advance of the event with approximate numbers.

  • Cancellation

    This deposit will be non-refundable if cancellation is within 2 weeks of your event; otherwise you will be entitled to a refund of 50% of your deposit.

  • Numbers

    If the numbers attending is greater than the maximum capacity of the room, Mr Ant’s will be under no obligation to provide facilities or cater for the additional numbers.

  • Payment

    Any outstanding payment for an event must be made in full at the end of the event. All major credit card details are accepted. All payment must be paid in pounds sterling and are subject to Value Added Tax at the current rate.

  • Timings

    Mr Ant’s is available for private hire at the times specified on our website. Use of the room outside the above times can be discussed and confirmed on a case by case basis. The function shall start and finish in accordance with the timings specified or agreed in writing.

  • Menus & Drinks List

    Menus are based on seasonal availability and are subject to change. We do cater for dietary requirements and kindly ask to be advised of these in advance. The drinks and wine list is subject to price and changes throughout the year. Any menu and drinks changes shall be relayed to the client. In order to fully prepare for your reservation we require menu choices to be confirmed 7 days prior to the date of the function. Only food produced by Mr Ant’s kitchen is permitted for consumption on the premises.

  • Mr Ant’s Premises

    The Client will ensure that:
    No damage is caused to Mr Ant’s, its fittings, decorations and furnishings. The conduct of its guests does not cause any nuisance or offence to other person or guests visiting Mr Ant’s. The client is to ensure that the guests do not access the bar after the event. Any items belonging to the restaurant remain on the premises. The guests fully comply with the emergency procedures should the need arise.

  • Age Restrictions

    We operate a strict policy on underage drinking. Guests that appear to be under 21 may be challenged for photo I.D. in an acceptable format. Acceptable formats include: driving licences, passports or pass approved ID cards. We reserve the right to refuse service if acceptable ID is not produced.

  • Pay Bar Customers – Licensing

    We are responsible for the operation of your bar in accordance with the Licensing Act 2003. This means that the premises used for the event, including marquees or private houses, will have been licensed to permit the retailing of alcohol for the duration of the event and the licensing law will be in operation. We will arrange a temporary event notice for you. There is a charge of £21. Payment for this is required when booking the bar service. If no such licence exists for the premises the bar will not be permitted to open.

Deposits will be retained in the event of cancellation of your booking with less than 7 days notice.

  • Access

    You will be required to ensure that we have access to the venue at the time we agree with you for the installation of your bar hire equipment. You must inform us in advance if there are restrictions to the venue access such as time restrictions, parking restrictions or car park height restrictions.

  • Equipment

    In the event of any specialist bar equipment being required it will be the responsibility of the hirer to meet the cost of such equipment.

  • Damage
